Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Q: What is the mission of Feast Forward Foundation?

    2. A: The mission of Feast Forward Foundation is to alleviate hunger and food insecurity by providing nutritious meals to individuals and families in need across our community.

  2. Q: How does Feast Forward Foundation source its food?

    3. A: We partner with local farms, grocery stores, restaurants, and food manufacturers to rescue surplus food that would otherwise go to waste. We also accept donations from individuals and organizations.

  3. Q: Who is eligible to receive assistance from Feast Forward Foundation?

    4. A: Feast Forward Foundation serves anyone facing food insecurity, regardless of age, gender, or background. We believe everyone deserves access to nutritious food when they need it most.

  4. Q: How can I get involved with Feast Forward Foundation as a volunteer?

    5. A: We welcome volunteers to help with various tasks such as food collection, meal preparation, distribution, and fundraising events. Visit our website or contact us directly to learn more about volunteer opportunities.

  5. Q: Does Feast Forward Foundation only provide food assistance, or do you offer other services as well?

    6. A: In addition to providing food assistance, Feast Forward Foundation may offer nutrition education programs, cooking classes, and referrals to other social services to help individuals and families improve their overall well-being.

  6. Q: How can I make a donation to support the work of Feast Forward Foundation?

    7. A: You can make a donation online through our website, by mail, or in person at one of our donation drop-off locations. We also accept food donations of non-perishable items and fresh produce.

  7. Q: Is Feast Forward Foundation a registered nonprofit organization?

    8. A: Yes, Feast Forward Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law.

  8. Q: How does Feast Forward Foundation ensure that donated food is safe to distribute?

    9. A: We adhere to strict food safety guidelines and regulations set forth by local health authorities. Our team inspects and properly handles all donated food items to ensure they meet safety standards before distribution.

  9. Q: What impact has Feast Forward Foundation had on the community since its inception?

    10. A: Since our inception, Feast Forward Foundation has provided millions of meals to individuals and families facing food insecurity in our community. Our programs have helped alleviate hunger and improve the overall health and well-being of those we serve.

  10. Q: How can I stay updated on the latest news and events from Feast Forward Foundation?

    11. A: You can stay connected with Feast Forward Foundation by following us on social media, subscribing to our newsletter, or visiting our website regularly. We frequently post updates about our programs, events, and volunteer opportunities.
